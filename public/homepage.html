What is Doll Face?

The only NFT project that stares back at you.

Doll Face is the brainchild of bishop, OG NFT artist, NFT Pioneer and purveyor of all things creepy and paranormal. Art by Lisa Fogarty, an extremely strong, fully famous NFT Artist, and legend in Cryptoart. Development by Adam Kagy, 20-year veteran software developer and smart contract specialist.

Doll Face is an NFT project with planned multimedia ecosystem revolving around the Ethereum blockchain. The goal of the project is to build a multimedia brand around the lore of the Doll Face set and setting.

Roadmap

We have a comprehensive plan for an entire generation of canonical multimedia which is able to interact and coexist together within the same universe contained on Ethereum.

  • Q1

    Jan 2022

    • Pre-mint: 1,000 Series 0 Dolls given out to early supporters.
    • Initial Mint: 3,500 Series 1 Dolls.
    • Promotional materials and community building.
    • Game Development Begins (contingent)

    Feb 2022

    • Hauntings begin
    • Amulets are forged
    • Lore expansion
    • Begin developing strategic partnerships

    Mar 2022

    • Release of our game currency $DOLL
    • Begin staking your Doll Face NFTs for $DOLL
    • $DOLL is not an investment; it is an ecosystem-specific utility token for interaction with and user-involved expansion of the Doll Face media and lore.
    • Begin developing strategic partnerships

  • Q2

    Apr 2022

    • Launch of dApp for user content creation.
    • Name your Doll Face for $DOLL
    • Create a backstory for your Doll Face, or have one written for you for $DOLL

    May 2022

    • Additional NFT Content.
    • Have your backstory narrated by a professional voice actor and purchase an NFT of your audiobook short story.

    Jun 2022

    • In-Game item creation and minting. Spend your $DOLL on in-game items and power ups
    • Marketplace development begins

  • Q3

    Aug 2022

    • Doll Market – Buy, Sell and Trade your Doll Faces, backstories, amulets and in-game items on our exclusive marketplace dApp, for $DOLL token or $ETH.

  • +

    Beyond

    • Media Content Production, games, books, audiobooks, and film.
